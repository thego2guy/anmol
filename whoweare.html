WHO WE ARE

ANMOL is in the business of “Unlocking The Power Of Nature” and making it accessible to the masses.

A premium one stop renewable energy developing company, with offices in Hyderabad and NCR, ANMOL creates and installs Solar power plants from concept to commissioning and accepts mandate to maintains them for 25 years. Founded by an Industry Veteran Mr. Anupam Mathur who has extensive experience of working across the globe, primarily in countries like Thailand, South America and India, ANMOL operates on his vision as a dedicated agency that provides customized optimum EPC solutions to rooftop and ground mounted projects through its unique open-book concept up to 25MW solar projects.

Our Mission

As a renewable energy business, our mission is to eradicate the dependence on high cost fossil-fuel based energy and replace it with an eco-friendly, efficacious measure by tapping the everpresent energy of The Sun.

Our Vision

Vision - Our Vision is to provide meaningful customized solutions for the ever-increasing electricity costs based on client needs. We want to be a preferred player in this space locally and eventually on a global scale.

Founder

With an experience of over 20+ years in the business of renewable energy powers, the Founder and CEO, Mr. Anupam Mathur has had a distinguished career leading ultra-large projects for Mytrah Energy, Adani Power, Suzlon, Inox and NSL. Hailing from Ghaziabad (Uttar Pradesh, India), he is an alumni and holds a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering from Punjab Engineering College. He has also completed a General Management course from IIM, Bangalore to sharpen his over all management and financial skillset.
